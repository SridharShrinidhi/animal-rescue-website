Adoption Application

Steps to follow for adoption:

  • Step 1: MEET THE DOGS

    Meet-and-greet for you to get to know the dog(s) and ask any questions you may have (temperament energy level, personality, etc).

  • Step 2: CHOOSE YOUR DOG

    Select the dog(s) you’d like to adopt here.

  • Step 3: SUBMIT THE ADOPTION APPLICATION FORM.

    Complete the adoption application.

  • Step 4: HOME CHECK

    After you have met the dog(s) and both you and BARS feel that you are a good fit for each other, we will set up a home check which ensures that your home and yard are animal friendly. (Home check can be done when we bring the dog to your home.)

  • Step 5: ADOPTION

    After the home check is completed, BARS will bring your new dog(s) to their new home! At this time, you will pay the adoption fee and sign the contract.

Each adoption costs $400 + HST. Includes vaccination and basic medical aid.
